About me

Built around real student routines

Study Mesh is a peer-to-peer platform where students can book affordable tutoring sessions, share revision notes, and build a profile that shows what they need help with and what they can teach.

Peer-to-peer

Learn from someone close to your level

Instead of only formal tuition, the mesh lets Primary, Secondary, Polytechnic, Junior College, and University students support one another with targeted sessions and practical notes.

After joining

Book sessions and buy notes

Once you sign up or log in, you can reserve tutor sessions, purchase study notes through PayNow, PayLah, or bank transfer, and edit your profile details anytime.
